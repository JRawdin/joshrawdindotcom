About Me

My story

  • March 1991

    Buffalo Beginnings

    In March of 1991 I was born into this world. I spent the first 17 years of my life in a suburb outside of Buffalo, New York. Because of this I am a die-hard fan of the Bills, Sabres, and chicken wings.

  • August 2009

    A Layover in Rochester

    After graduating high school, I attended college at St. John Fisher College in Rochester, New York. Here I majored in Sports Management and played on the Division III football team at the school. After a year at St. John Fisher I determined it wasn't for me and transferred out.

  • August 2010

    Spartan for Life

    I transferred from St. John Fisher to Michigan State University to begin my sophomore year. MSU became a place I loved just as much as Buffalo. I graduated in May 2013 with a degree in marketing and a specialization in Information Technology from the Eli Broad College of Business.

  • July 2013

    A Career is Launched

    I began my professional life in July 2013 working for Whirlpool Corporation in St. Joseph, Michigan. I am a member of Whirlpool's Global Information Systems Leadership Development Program. This rotational program is meant to develop exceptional talent into leaders.

Christina Lalama Logo

Logo design for teacher portfolio

I developed a logo and accompanying banner images for Christina Lalama's teaching portfolio. This portfolio is part of her capstone project at Central Michigan University. I developed a simple, but effective logo for her to use across all media outlets. I used a pencil in the logo because it is a universal symbol for learning and teaching The elegant CL initials add a classy touch to bring it together. The logo and wordmarks use the Blyth font created by Nick Slater.

Original Personal Site

My first attempt at web development

This is the original version of my personal website, the first site I ever coded. I build the site using Bootstrap 3, WordPress, Font Awesome, Subtle Patterns, flatuicolors.com, StackExchange, Codecademy, and DreamHost.

I chose to design my site using a flat UI because it results in a clean, minimalist site that is easy to read and follow. I also made joshrawdin.com responsive. With the proliferation of mobile devices, it was important to me that my site perform well across any device.

Snapchat Geofilters

Local graphics for Snapchat

These graphics were created for Snapchat's Geofilter feature. This allows users to add a filter to their images based on their current location. I made a filter for the two areas where I live and work, St. Joseph and Benton Harbor.

The St. Joseph geofilter was inspired by the lighthouse on Lake Michigan. This lighthouse is the most notable landmark in the small beach town. THe blue and yellow in the graphic come from the colors of St. Joseph High School.

The Benton Harbor geofilter was inspired by the local art installations by ARS Art & Culture Center titled "I Am The Greatest." You can more about this project here. The orange is not only the color of these sculptures, but also the primary color of Benton Harbor High School.
